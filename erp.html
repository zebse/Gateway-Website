Enterprise Resource Planning

Enterprise resource planning is the integrated management of main business processes, often in real time and mediated by software and technology. ERP system cover the below main components or modules.

Accounting

Take total control of your company’s finances in real-time with the Accounting module. Manage customers, vendors, sales, expenses, bank accounts and various reports. Generate live and detailed reports, make transactions, generate invoices, and pay employees from one place.

HR Management

The employee listing feature lets you add all the personal details of an employee in one place. Keep assessment reports, complaints, increments & every possible detail you need! New employees, designation, department, reporting, and leave management are right at your fingertips. Manage everything professionally with our complete HR Solution.

CRM

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software are powerful tools for a business or enterprise to use. CRM handles sales, marketing, and customer service information. It handles activities such as recording customer interactions, sales tracking, pipeline management, prospecting, and creating/evaluating marketing campaigns.

Inventory

You will be able to see a complete overview of all the stocked products that you have along with the item code, buying/selling price, number of stocks left, and more. Automatically generate in-detailed reports with the simple click of a button. Whether you want instant purchase/product or complete inventory report.

Training

Ensure all the necessary training for all of your employees. Upload & access all your training resources in one place. No more hard copy is necessary. With training credit, hours, and trainer’s notes, keep track of all the performance without any hassle. Employees can view their completed, current, or upcoming training to always stay updated

Other modules

In addition to the mentioned modules, ERP support many other modules like Workflow, Recruitment, Attendance, document manager, SMS notification, and many more!
